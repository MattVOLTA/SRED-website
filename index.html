Why it exists

Built for the founder who cannot afford a year-end archaeology project

Most early-stage software teams know the hard technical work when it happens. By filing season, the source links, eval failures, tradeoffs, and approvals are scattered across tools. This repo gives the team a small operating rhythm before that context disappears.

01

During the sprint

Capture technical uncertainty, experiments, failures, model evals, decisions, and the evidence that proves when they happened.

02

During the month

Review what is accepted, excluded, weak, blocked, sensitive, or waiting on finance, privacy, CTO, or advisor input.

03

Before advisor review

Hand over a clean binder with source-backed narratives, cost support, privacy decisions, approval status, and unresolved questions.

What it produces

A source-linked workspace that can survive scrutiny

Each artifact exists to answer a question the claim process will eventually ask. The repo keeps facts, interpretation, approvals, and sensitive raw material in separate lanes.

Claim question index

The founder control board for what is answered, blocked, excluded, or waiting for human review.

Evidence index and cards

Dated artifacts from Git, tickets, evals, docs, meetings, logs, finance, and payroll stay traceable.

Experiment ledger

Hypotheses, attempts, tests, results, and conclusions stay tied to the technical unknown being investigated.

AI eval register

Models, prompts, datasets, baselines, thresholds, metrics, traces, and failure patterns are captured while fresh.

Time and finance ledgers

Final review binder

An advisor-facing index of approved facts, draft narratives, evidence, costs, privacy status, and open questions.

Founder guardrails

Useful evidence support, not tax advice

This operating system helps a startup preserve what happened and prepare materials for qualified review. It does not decide final eligibility, calculate a credit, promise a refund, file a claim, or replace a CPA or SR&ED specialist.

Technical leadApproves technical facts and experiment conclusions.
Finance ownerApproves source financial records and effort inputs.
Privacy ownerApproves redaction, export, and customer evidence handling.
